#  Natural Language Processing Pipeline

| Step | Process | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| 1 | Tokenization | Split text into words or tokens |
| 2 | Lowercasing | Convert text to lowercase |
| 3 | Stop Words Removal | Remove common words (task dependent) |
| 4 | Lemmatization | Convert words to their base form |
| 5 | Feature Extraction | Convert text into numerical vectors |
| 6 | Model Training | Feed vectors into ML/DL model |
| 7 | Prediction | Classification, Translation, Summarization, etc. |

---

## Feature Extraction Techniques

- Bag of Words (BoW)
- TF-IDF
- Word2Vec
- GloVe
- FastText
- BERT Embeddings
- Sentence Transformers

---

## Final Workflow

```text
Raw Text
    │
    ▼

    │
    ▼

    │
    ▼

    │
    ▼
Prediction
```
